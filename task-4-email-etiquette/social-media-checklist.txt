5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student

5 Doís for responsible social media use as a college student:
* Use strong privacy settings to protect personal information.
* Share academic or professional achievements responsibly.
* Verify information before reposting or sharing.
* Engage respectfully in discussions and debates.
* Maintain a positive digital footprint for future opportunities.

5 Dontís for responsible social media use as a college student:
    *  Post offensive or discriminatory content.
* Share confidential academic or personal details publicly.
* Engage in cyberbullying or negative comments.
* Overshare personal life events that may harm your reputation.
* Use social media during class or professional meetings.
