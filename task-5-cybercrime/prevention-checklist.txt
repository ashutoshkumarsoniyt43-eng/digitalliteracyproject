“Stay Safe Online” checklist with at least 8 specific, actionable prevention tips relevant to college students in India and two tips must be specific to financial/UPI safety.

Least 8 specific, actionable prevention tips relevant to college students in India:
* Never enter your UPI PIN when someone claims to “send” you money.
* Verify payment requests carefully before approving.
* Use official apps only (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM).
* Enable transaction alerts via SMS/email.
* Keep banking apps updated.
* Report suspicious activity immediately.
* Educate peers about UPI scams.
* Regularly check account balances.

Financial Safety Tips:
* Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords.
* Use daily transaction limits to minimize risk.
Reporting Channels:
* National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline: 1930
